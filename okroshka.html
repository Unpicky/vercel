Okroshka Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3–4 boiled potatoes
  • 3 boiled eggs
  • 300 g cooked meat or sausage
  • 3–4 fresh cucumbers
  • 5–6 radishes
  • Green onions to taste
  • Fresh dill and parsley to taste
  • About 1 liter of kefir, whey, or kvass
  • Sour cream for serving
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Dice the potatoes, eggs, cucumbers, radishes, and meat/sausage into small cubes.
  2. Add chopped green onions and fresh herbs.
  3. Mix everything well in a large bowl.
  4. Before serving, pour in kefir, whey, or kvass — whichever you prefer.
  5. Add a spoonful of sour cream and season lightly with salt and pepper.

Tips

  • For a lighter version, mix kefir with sparkling mineral water.
  • For a classic taste, use kvass.
  • Okroshka is versatile, refreshing, and full of flavor. Try it once — and it may become your favorite summer recipe!
